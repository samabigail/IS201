Yosemite: A Fantastic National Park


I have visited Yosemite National Park many times over my life, and every time I go it is an unforgettable experience. Let me share with you some of the reasons that you should take the time to visit Yosemite National Park.

  1. Beautiful Scenery
    • Glacier Point is one of my personal favorites!
  2. Fantastic Hikes
    • There's an incredible range of hikes from really easy to difficult
  3. Stunning Waterfalls
    • Nevada Falls
    • Upper and Lower Yosemite Falls
    • Bridalveil Falls
    • Many more!
  4. Awesome Campgrounds
    • There are also cabins or a Yosemite Valley Lodge if camping isn't your cup of tea
  5. Great Food
    • Pizza
    • Ice cream!!
    • Burgers!
    • There's also a great general store
  6. Sometimes Bears!
